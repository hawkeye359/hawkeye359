About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to make kanpur the highest selection giving city in defence sector. We are continuously imporoving our quality of education both in terms of theory and physical. We want to provide best in class study material and support to every student. We want to provide the highest standard study environment to our students. Our academy emphasizes on all-round development of the cadets so that they consistently strive hard to live up to the expectations of their parents and nation at large.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide best quality education even to the lowest class of sociey. We provide incentives so that the even the financially weak students can get high quality education. Even though not possible,we try our best to ensure that every student of our coaching is able to qualify the defence exam. We monitor every student individually to ensure that they are understanding the concepts thoroughly and not left behind. We are committed to give our students the best education and training, so that they are ready to face the challenges of the exam environment.

Director's Message

       The guiding philosophies of the Institute throughout have been the creation of knowledge and integration globally. The objectives of imparting education, combined with creation, dissemination, and application of knowledge, are being met in an integrated form, to create a synergetic impact.
       Universal Defence Academy has been offering educational services to the students preparing for the Defence exams like NDA/NA, Airforce, Navy SSR & other exams.
       The institute imparts a comprehensive training-cum-guidance program. The Institute was established to provide a complete solution to the students preparing for the exam. The focus is on providing holistic education not only covering the prescribed syllabus but also enabling the students to face other challenges of life with equal ease. It inculcates human values and professional ethics in the students, which help them make decisions and create a path that is good not only for them, but also are good for society, for the nation, and the world as a whole.
       To fulfill its mission in new and powerful ways, each student of Universal Defence Academy is given quality education and training so that they can cope up with the competition in the market and prove themselves before everyone. We always believe in setting the trail, and not in following the path.
